About Me

Designer who also codes.

"Design is not just what it looks like — it's how it works."

Hi, I'm Jesse Mulure — a seasoned graphic designer and full-stack developer based in Kenya. I bridge the gap between stunning aesthetics and functional technology, delivering work that doesn't just look great but performs.

With expertise spanning Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Blender, CorelDraw, Figma, WordPress, and modern web technologies (HTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL, JavaScript), I bring a rare combination of creative and technical skills to every project.

I'm also an active member of a media production team, delivering high-quality sound and video editing, as well as seamless live streaming experiences.
